### Idea 1: "Quiet Warmth" — Warm Minimal Editorial

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Warm Minimalism / Film Photography Aesthetic

**Core Principles:**
- Photographs are the hero — all UI is subordinate to imagery
- Generous whitespace creates a gallery-like calm
- Warmth through color temperature, not decoration
- Asymmetric layouts break monotony without adding noise

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: warm off-white (#FAF7F4) — not pure white, slightly warm
- Text: deep warm charcoal (#2C2420) — not black, has warmth
- Accent: dusty rose / blush (#C9A99A) — subtle, feminine, warm
- Secondary: warm sand (#E8DDD4) for section dividers and cards
- Emotional intent: feels like holding a printed photograph in your hands

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero with centered text overlay (dark text on light image)
- Portfolio grid: asymmetric masonry-style, mixing portrait and landscape crops
- About section: split layout — text left, large portrait right (offset, not centered)
- Navigation: minimal top bar, left-aligned logo, right-aligned links

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule lines (1px, warm grey) as section dividers
- Serif display font for headings, creates editorial elegance
- Subtle grain/noise texture overlay on hero image

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover on portfolio images: gentle scale (1.02) + brightness lift
- Navigation links: underline slide-in from left on hover
- Smooth scroll between sections

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up on scroll for text blocks (subtle, 0.4s)
- Hero text: fade in on load with slight upward drift
- Portfolio images: staggered fade-in on scroll

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — headings, hero title
- Body: Lato (sans-serif) — body text, navigation, captions
- Hierarchy: 56px hero title / 32px section headers / 18px body / 13px captions
